How generalizable is SW research?

  1. We do not take the true variety of Linked Data into account.
  2. Our results are less generelizable than they should be.
  3. Using many datasets in our evaluations is hard.

Why are Web-Scale Evaluations Difficult?

  1. Datasets are hard to find.
  2. Many datasets are ‘messy’.
  3. Datasets are not accessible via a uniform interface.
